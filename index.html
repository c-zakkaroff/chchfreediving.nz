Christchurch Freediving Club

We are a freediving club in Christchurch, Canterbury, New Zealand. We train weekly at Graham Condon swimming pool in north Christchurch. We provide a safe and welcoming environment for all freedivers, whether you're new to the sport or want to train for international competition. To join the club, we hold inductions every month.

Freediving is the sport of diving while holding your breath (apnea). In competition, there are pool and depth disciplines: in the pool divers try to maximise either distance (dynamic apnea) or time (static apnea).

People train in the pool for lots of reasons: for fun, for competition, to improve their spearfishing or deep freediving, or to be more confident when snorkelling, diving, or surfing.
